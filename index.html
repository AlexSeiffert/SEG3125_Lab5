About Alex Cycle Shop

Alex Cycle Shop started in 2016 as a weekend repair booth near the Ottawa bike paths. Today we focus on clear diagnostics, upfront pricing, and safe repairs.

Core working ethics
  • Transparent pricing: approve changes before work starts.
  • Safety first: brakes/wheels/drivetrain checks prioritized.
  • Respect your time: predictable turnaround.
